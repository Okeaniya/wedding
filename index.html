Wish

Your mother

May this day always have a special meaning for you, our dear children. We wish you to be happy next to each other, under a peaceful sky. May adversity bypass your doorstep, may your family be bright and joyful!

Your sister Jana and her family

My dear sister! Congratulations on your wedding day! I would like to wish you a simple woman's happiness - you deserve it. Let everything go well, be happy together. I wish you to turn your dreams into reality, to travel, to develop. It is very difficult for me to part with you, because you are my closest person. I am grateful to you for all your advice: it has helped me out more than once. Thank you for being with me! I sincerely rejoice in your happiness!

Your sister Natalie

Sister! On your wedding day, I heartily and sincerely congratulate you! May your family life be joyful, happy, beautiful, and bright. May respect and understanding prevail in your relations. May the hearth of your home, which you will certainly light, never go out. May there be many guests and joyful laughter in your home.
